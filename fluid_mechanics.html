Fluid Mechanics - Physics

Subtopics of Fluid Mechanics with Key Formulas
Subtopic Main Concepts / Equations
Fluid Properties Density: ρ = m/V
Pressure: P = F/A
Specific gravity: SG = ρ_fluid / ρ_water
Viscosity: η = F / (A × velocity gradient)
Hydrostatics Pressure in fluid: P = P₀ + ρgh
Pascal's Law: ΔP applied = transmitted everywhere
Buoyant force: F_b = ρ_fluid × V_displaced × g
Archimedes’ principle: Object immersed experiences upward force
Fluid Flow Continuity equation: A₁v₁ = A₂v₂
Flow rate: Q = Av
Bernoulli’s equation: P + ½ρv² + ρgh = constant
Laminar vs Turbulent flow: Reynolds number Re = ρvD/η
Viscosity and Drag Stokes' Law: F = 6πηrv
Drag force in fluids: F_d = ½ C_d ρ A v²
Terminal velocity: v_t = mg / 6πηr (small sphere)
Surface Tension Surface tension: γ = F/L
Capillary rise: h = 2γ cosθ / (ρgr)
Work done: W = γ ΔA
Compressible Fluids (Optional) Speed of sound in fluid: v = √(B/ρ), B = bulk modulus
Ideal gas flow: PV^γ = constant (adiabatic)
