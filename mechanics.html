Mechanics - Physics

Subtopics of Mechanics with Key Formulas
Subtopic Main Concepts / Equations
Kinematics Displacement: s = ut + ½at²
Final velocity: v = u + at
v² = u² + 2as
Average velocity: v_avg = (u + v)/2
Acceleration: a = (v - u)/t
Dynamics Newton's 1st Law: Inertia principle
Newton's 2nd Law: F = ma
Newton's 3rd Law: F₁₂ = -F₂₁
Friction: f = μN
Centripetal force: F_c = mv²/r
Work, Energy, and Power Work: W = F · d · cosθ
Kinetic Energy: KE = ½mv²
Potential Energy: PE = mgh
Mechanical Energy: E = KE + PE
Power: P = W/t
Momentum and Collisions Momentum: p = mv
Impulse: J = FΔt = Δp
Conservation: m₁u₁ + m₂u₂ = m₁v₁ + m₂v₂
Elastic collision: KE conserved
Inelastic collision: KE not conserved
Rotational Mechanics Torque: τ = r × F
Moment of inertia: I = Σmr²
Angular acceleration: α = τ/I
Angular momentum: L = Iω
Rotational kinetic energy: KE_rot = ½ Iω²
Gravitation Newton’s law of gravitation: F = G(m₁m₂)/r²
Gravitational potential energy: U = -G(m₁m₂)/r
Acceleration due to gravity: g = GM/R²
Orbital speed: v = √(GM/r)
Fluid Mechanics Pressure: P = F/A
Density: ρ = m/V
Buoyant force: F_b = ρVg
Continuity: A₁v₁ = A₂v₂
Bernoulli’s principle: P + ½ρv² + ρgh = constant
Oscillations Displacement: x = A sin(ωt + φ)
Angular frequency: ω = 2π/T
Period of spring: T = 2π√(m/k)
Period of pendulum: T = 2π√(l/g)
Energy: E = ½ k A²
