Optics - Physics

Subtopics of Optics with Key Formulas
Subtopic Main Concepts / Equations
Reflection of Light Law of reflection: Angle of incidence = Angle of reflection
Plane mirrors: Image distance = Object distance (v = u)
Magnification: m = height of image / height of object
Refraction of Light Snell's Law: n₁ sinθ₁ = n₂ sinθ₂
Refractive index: n = c/v
Critical angle: sinθ_c = n₂/n₁
Total internal reflection: occurs for θ > θ_c
Lenses Lens formula: 1/f = 1/v − 1/u
Magnification: m = v/u
Convex lens: converging, real/virtual images
Concave lens: diverging, virtual images
Mirror and Lens Combinations Use lens and mirror formula: 1/f = 1/v − 1/u
Sign convention: real positive, virtual negative
Magnification: product of individual magnifications
Optical Instruments Microscope: Magnification M = m_o × m_e
Telescope: Angular magnification: M = θ' / θ
Camera: Lens formula 1/f = 1/v − 1/u
Wave Optics Interference: Path difference = nλ (constructive), (n+½)λ (destructive)
Young's double slit: Fringe width β = λL/d
Diffraction: Angular width θ ≈ λ/a
Polarization: Transmission through polarizer: I = I₀ cos²θ
Dispersion and Spectra Dispersion: Separation of light into colors by prism
Refractive index varies with wavelength: n = c/v(λ)
Rainbow: Caused by refraction, reflection, and dispersion
