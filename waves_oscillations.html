Waves and Oscillations - Physics

Subtopics of Waves and Oscillations with Key Formulas
Subtopic Main Concepts / Equations
Mechanical Waves Wave speed: v = λf
Frequency: f = 1/T
Period: T = 1/f
Types: Transverse, Longitudinal
Wave equation: y(x,t) = A sin(kx − ωt)
Sound Waves Speed of sound in air: v = 331 + 0.6T (m/s)
Sound intensity: I = P/A
Decibel level: β = 10 log₁₀(I/I₀)
Fundamental frequency of string: f₁ = v/2L
Simple Harmonic Motion (SHM) Displacement: x = A sin(ωt + φ)
Velocity: v = dx/dt = ω√(A² − x²)
Acceleration: a = −ω²x
Angular frequency: ω = 2π/T = √(k/m)
Period: T = 2π√(m/k) (spring), T = 2π√(l/g) (pendulum)
Energy in Oscillations Kinetic energy: KE = ½ mω²(A² − x²)
Potential energy: PE = ½ k x²
Total energy: E = ½ k A² = constant
Resonance Resonance occurs when driving frequency = natural frequency
Maximum amplitude at resonance
Used in musical instruments, tuning forks
Superposition and Interference Principle: Resultant displacement = sum of individual displacements
Constructive interference: waves in phase
Destructive interference: waves out of phase
Standing wave: λ_n = 2L/n (string fixed at both ends)
Doppler Effect Observed frequency: f' = f (v ± v_o)/(v ∓ v_s)
v = speed of sound, v_o = observer speed, v_s = source speed
