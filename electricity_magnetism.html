Subtopic Main Concepts / Equations
Electrostatics Coulomb's Law: F = k (q₁q₂)/r²
Electric field: E = F/q = kQ/r²
Electric potential: V = kQ/r
Potential energy: U = k q₁q₂/r
Current and Circuits Ohm's Law: V = IR
Resistance: R = ρL/A
Power: P = VI = I²R = V²/R
Series: R_eq = R₁ + R₂ + ...
Parallel: 1/R_eq = 1/R₁ + 1/R₂ + ...
Capacitance Capacitance: C = Q/V
Energy stored: U = ½ CV²
Series: 1/C_eq = 1/C₁ + 1/C₂ + ...
Parallel: C_eq = C₁ + C₂ + ...
Magnetism Magnetic field due to a current: B = μ₀I/(2πr)
Force on moving charge: F = q(v × B)
Lorentz force: F = q(E + v × B)
Biot–Savart Law: dB = (μ₀/4π)(I dl × r̂)/r²
Electromagnetic Induction Faraday’s Law: ε = −dΦ_B/dt
Lenz's Law: Induced current opposes change in flux
Inductance: L = NΦ/I
Energy stored: U = ½ LI²
AC and DC Circuits AC voltage: v = V₀ sin(ωt)
RMS value: I_rms = I₀/√2, V_rms = V₀/√2
Impedance: Z = √(R² + (X_L − X_C)²)

Speed of EM waves in vacuum: c = 3 × 10⁸ m/s
Relation: c² = 1/(ε₀μ₀)
